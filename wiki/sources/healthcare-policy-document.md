## Summary
This document establishes the framework for managing patient data, emphasizing HIPAA compliance, strict access controls, and specific data retention periods.

## Key Claims
- All patient data handling must comply with HIPAA regulations.
- Medical records require a minimum 7-year retention period.
- Data breaches must be reported within 24 hours.
- Staff must undergo annual privacy training.

## Key Quotes
> "All patient data must be handled in compliance with HIPAA regulations." — Patient Privacy section
> "Medical records must be retained for a minimum of 7 years after the last patient encounter." — Data Retention section
