# Wiki Overview

This wiki serves as a living synthesis of healthcare policies, technical frameworks, and organizational knowledge.

## Data Management & Privacy

Current healthcare policy emphasizes strict adherence to [[HIPAA]] to ensure [[PatientPrivacy]]. Key requirements include a 7-year [[DataRetention]] period for medical records and daily electronic backups. Security is maintained through rigorous [[AccessControl]], where only authorized providers have access, supported by audit logs and annual staff training. The [[PrivacyOffice]] serves as the central authority for policy enforcement and breach reporting, which must occur within 24 hours of an incident.
